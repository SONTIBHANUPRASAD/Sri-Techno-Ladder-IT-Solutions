About Us

We are pleased to introduce ourselves as one of the leading Training Institutes in Guntur District. Sri Techno Ladder IT Solutions is an Educational Place specializing in IT-Education & Technology Sector, established by professionals with vision to develop budding professionals, in right blend of IT Education.

We are in to training and developing Academic Projects for B.Tech., M.Tech., and M.C.A Students. We also developed websites for various Engineering colleges and Degree colleges.

Sri Techno Ladder IT Solutions has earned a well-deserved reputation for its track record and with student satisfaction. Our strength of proficient execution is backed by experienced and efficient faculty. We are an ISO 9001:200IS Certified institute. We also have fully equipped Labs with over 50 computers.

To enrich the knowledge & skill sets of professional students by providing value added training in the areas of Software Development & Testing. To serve the industries by providing trained human resources in the above areas.

To impart Quality Computer Education among each and every class of students. The purpose, the aim and drive of our institute is to equip the student with the most excellent computer proficiency.
